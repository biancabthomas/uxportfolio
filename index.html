About Me

Hi my name is Bianca. Welcome to my second website UX Design Process. This website focuses mainly on User Experience (UX) Design. My first website shows a little of my UX Design work, so with this website, you get more insight of the UX Design Projects.

Here is a little backstory about me. I am a George Mason University Alumni with a Bachelor's degree in Individualized Study and a minor in Web Design. One of the key points about me is I like challenging myself on projects I have never done before. As I explored more about UX, I have done my research to learn more about UX Design.
